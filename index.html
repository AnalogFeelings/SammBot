Samm-Bot

A simple Discord bot packed with a couple of nice features.

Samm-Bot by AestheticalZ

A simple bot.

Samm-Bot is a bot that focuses on being simple to use.
Whether you want to invite it to your server for fun, or for moderation purposes, Samm-Bot will be unintrusive and slick to use.

No fuss at all.

No ads for some sort of premium subscription.
No random pings telling you that you levelled up.
Samm-Bot is a very quiet bot that will only interact with you when you interact with it.

Has all the basics.

Samm-Bot is a bot has all the basics you need to moderate your server.
Welcome messages, warning system, muting, logging, and more to come in future updates.

Open source, too.

Samm-Bot is built on .NET technologies, and it is open source.
It always has been, and will always be.
The code is hosted on GitHub, and everyone is able to contribute to its development.
